Rock, Paper, Scissors

Choose a game mode:

Beat the CPU in a classic game of Rock, Paper, Scissors. You will specify the amount of rounds that are to be played.

Earn points based on selecting the correct answer as the CPU tells you to win or lose against their choice. 10 points are rewarded for correct answers and -20 points for incorrect answers. You will have 30 seconds to get as many points as possible. Good luck!

Play classic Rock, Paper, Scissors but while placing bets at the beginning of each round. You will start out with $100, but be careful: if the round results in a tie, you must bet double the amount from the previous round.
